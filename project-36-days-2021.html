36 Days of Type 2021

The idea of merging typography and modern architecture is something that I have been playing around with since I was in college. The 36 days of Type challenge lent itself nicely to revisiting and creating all new, more refined letterforms. To engage users, each post was created using a different colour background from a larger gradient scale. When viewed as a whole the user would expereicnce a transitioning gradient of colour when scrolling the instagram account.
