Howell Plumbing

Ben Howell is an independent plumber serving the Muskoka area. He approached me wanting a logo created for his newly founded plumbing business.

The bold and recognizable design emphasizes the company's name, while the cleverly designed 'H' icon, shaped like the lines and curves of pipes, gives a nod to the business's area of expertise. The 'H' icon is not only unique but also reinforces the company's identity, making it easily recognizable to potential customers. Overall, the Howell Plumbing logo is a timeless representation of the business and is sure to leave a lasting impression on those who come across it.
