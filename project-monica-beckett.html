Monica Beckett Rebrand

Monica is an interior designer from Toronto, Canada. She wanted her new brand to help her stand out from other interior designers in North America. A simple brand identity was created to live beside and help showcase her work when being presented within her portfolio. The new brand suits her clean style and approach to interior design. The logotype and layouts created are modular and can be adapted to whatever medium she uses them in, whether it be print or digital. The site was coded by me in order to help the brand image stay strong when being viewed on web.
